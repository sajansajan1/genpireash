## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit Genpire.com** and click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
2. **Choose your role:**
   - **Creator** - For designers, brands, and retailers
   - **Supplier** - For manufacturers and production facilities

3. **Complete your profile:**
   - Add your company information
   - Upload a profile picture or logo
   - Set your preferences and categories

### Understanding Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:
- **Tech Pack Library** - All your generated tech packs
- **Active RFQs** - Pending quotation requests
- **Messages** - Communication with suppliers
- **Analytics** - Performance metrics and insights

## Creating Your First Tech Pack

### Step 1: Start Generation

Click the **"Generate Your Techpack"** button from your dashboard or homepage.

### Step 2: Choose Input Method

#### Option A: Text Description
Perfect for when you have a clear idea in mind.

**Example prompt:**
\`\`\`
Create a tech pack for a minimalist gold bracelet with:
- 14K gold material
- Simple chain design
- Lobster clasp closure
- 18cm length
- Polished finish
- Gift box packaging
\`\`\`

#### Option B: Upload Sketch
Upload your hand-drawn or digital sketches.

**Supported formats:** PNG, JPG, JPEG (Max 10MB)

#### Option C: Reference Images
Upload existing product photos for inspiration.

### Step 3: Customize Specifications

After AI generation, you can customize:

#### Materials
- Review suggested materials
- Add alternatives
- Adjust sustainability scores
- Specify suppliers if known

#### Dimensions
- Modify measurements
- Add size variations
- Include grading rules

#### Colors
- Select Pantone colors
- Add color variations
- Specify finish types

#### Packaging
- Choose packaging materials
- Define dimensions
- Add branding requirements

### Step 4: Generate Visual Assets

The AI will create:
- Front view rendering
- Back view (if applicable)
- Detail shots
- Technical drawings

**Tip:** You can regenerate any image by clicking "Regenerate" if not satisfied.

### Step 5: Review & Finalize

1. **Review all sections** for accuracy
2. **Add production notes** for special requirements
3. **Set quality standards** and testing requirements
4. **Save or Export** your tech pack

### Export Options

- **PDF** - Professional document for manufacturers
- **Excel** - Detailed spreadsheet format
- **Share Link** - Shareable link for collaborators
- **Send to Suppliers** - Direct RFQ creation

## Working with Suppliers

### Finding Suppliers

1. **Navigate to Supplier Marketplace**
2. **Use filters to narrow down:**
   - Location (domestic/international)
   - Product categories
   - MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)
   - Certifications
   - Lead times

3. **Review supplier profiles:**
   - Manufacturing capabilities
   - Portfolio samples
   - Client reviews
   - Response time

### Sending RFQs (Request for Quotation)

1. **Select your tech pack**
2. **Click "Send to Suppliers"**
3. **Fill in RFQ details:**
   \`\`\`
   Title: Gold Bracelet Manufacturing - 500 units
   Timeline: 6-8 weeks
   Quantity: 500 pieces
   Target Price: $70-80 per unit
   Special Requirements: Need sample before bulk production
   \`\`\`
4. **Select suppliers** (recommend 3-5 for comparison)
5. **Send RFQ**

### Evaluating Quotes

When suppliers respond, compare:
- **Sample cost** and timeline
- **Unit pricing** and volume discounts
- **MOQ** requirements
- **Lead time** for production
- **Payment terms**
- **Shipping options**
- **Quality guarantees**

### Communication Best Practices

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Better Tech Pack Generation

1. **Be Specific** in your descriptions
   \`\`\`
   Good: "Oversized cotton hoodie with dropped shoulders, 400gsm fleece, kangaroo pocket"
   Bad: "Make a hoodie"
   \`\`\`

2. **Include measurements** when known
3. **Specify target market** (men's, women's, children's)
4. **Mention budget constraints** upfront
5. **Add reference images** for style accuracy

### For Successful Supplier Relationships

1. **Communicate clearly** and promptly
2. **Respect MOQ requirements**
3. **Be realistic about timelines**
4. **Always request samples**
5. **Document all agreements**
6. **Build long-term partnerships**

### Cost Optimization Tips

1. **Order larger quantities** for better unit pricing
2. **Consider alternative materials** suggested by AI
3. **Group orders** with similar products
4. **Plan for seasonal production** schedules
5. **Negotiate payment terms** for cash flow

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Tech Pack Generation Issues

**Problem:** AI doesn't understand my product
**Solution:** Add more details, use industry-standard terms, upload reference images

**Problem:** Generated images don't match vision
**Solution:** Use "Regenerate" option with refined prompts

**Problem:** Missing specifications
**Solution:** Manually add in the edit mode

#### Supplier Communication

**Problem:** No responses to RFQs
**Solution:** 
- Check your requirements are realistic
- Expand supplier selection
- Improve RFQ clarity
- Adjust budget expectations

**Problem:** Language barriers
**Solution:** 
- Use simple, clear English
- Include visual references
- Confirm understanding on key points

#### Order Issues

**Problem:** Sample doesn't match tech pack
**Solution:**
- Document discrepancies with photos
- Request specific corrections
- Consider tech pack adjustments if minor

**Problem:** Production delays
**Solution:**
- Maintain regular communication
- Have backup suppliers
- Build buffer time in planning

## Glossary

**Tech Pack** - Technical package with all specifications for manufacturing
**MOQ** - Minimum Order Quantity required by supplier
**RFQ** - Request for Quotation sent to suppliers
**Lead Time** - Time from order to delivery
**Sample** - Prototype for approval before bulk production
**FOB** - Free on Board (pricing excludes shipping)
**CIF** - Cost, Insurance, and Freight (all-inclusive pricing)
**Pantone** - Standardized color matching system
**GSM** - Grams per Square Meter (fabric weight)
**CAD** - Computer-Aided Design drawings
